Car A is 15 m away from car B and moving to the right at 2.0 m/s, when the driver decides to bump into car B, which is at rest.

Car A accelerates at 1.5m/s2 to a speed of 5.0 m s and then continues at constant velocity until it strikes car B. Calculate the total time for car A to travel the 15 m.
